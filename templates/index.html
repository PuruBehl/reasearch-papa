Unlock all the secrets in your research paper

Research papa gives you a better understanding to the research paper you wanna read

Steps of how this works

Step 1

Upload the pdf file :
We recommend you adding a pdf format file . File size shouldn't be too big (maximmum 5 mb)

Step 2

Have a look at the seperated sections
These sections are distributed so you can understand the paper in a better manner. Give your opinion about the section in the text boxes provided to get a better understanding.

Step 3

Now you have done all you can . Last step is to have a look at what others thought about the research paper. We will provide u review of sections from other users if the paper is in our database. Making your understanding a bit better by giving you a out of the box opinions from people all over the world.
